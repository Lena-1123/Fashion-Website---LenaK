Chanel

Chanel Elegance

Chanel represents timeless beauty, sophistication, and classic Parisian fashion.

  • Founder

    Gabrielle Bonheur "Coco" Chanel was a French fashion designer and businesswoman. The founder and namesake of the Chanel brand, she was credited in the post–World War I era with popularising a sporty, casual chic as the feminine standard of style.


  • Brand Info

    Chanel is a French luxury fashion house founded by Coco Chanel in 1910. The brand is famous for elegant fashion, perfumes, handbags, and timeless designs. Today Chanel is one of the most influential fashion brands in the world.


  • Runways

    Chanel runway shows are known for their sophisticated atmosphere and creative stage designs. The brand often presents classic collections using black, white, and tweed fabrics.



  • Signature Items

    Some iconic Chanel items include the Chanel No.5 perfume, the classic tweed jacket, and the Chanel 2.55 handbag.
