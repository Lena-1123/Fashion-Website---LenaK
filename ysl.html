YSL Modern Edge

YSL combines bold style with modern elegance and powerful runway aesthetics.

  • Founder

    Yves Henri Donat Mathieu-Saint-Laurent or YSL, was a French fashion designer who, in 1962, founded his eponymous fashion label. He is regarded as being among the foremost fashion designers of the twentieth century.


  • Brand Info

    Yves Saint Laurent, also known as YSL, is a French luxury fashion house founded in 1961. The brand is known for modern designs and elegant tailoring.


  • Runways

    YSL runway shows often present bold and modern fashion with strong silhouettes. The brand mixes elegance with edgy and contemporary style..



  • Signature Items

    Some famous YSL items include the Le Smoking tuxedo suit, YSL logo bags, luxury perfumes, and cosmetics.
